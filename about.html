Meet the Creator

Hello, I'm Ching Nam Wong, a web developer in Seneca College.

My love for storytelling and creativity drew me to Dixit, as it seamlessly combines the joy of gameplay with the magic of imagination. The game's unique concept of using abstract and surreal artwork as the basis for storytelling sparked my curiosity from the very beginning.

I have always believed that games have the power to bring people together, and Dixit does just that. Its social nature and engaging gameplay make it a perfect choice for gatherings with friends and family, creating cherished memories with each play.

Why Dixit?

Dixit, designed by Jean-Louis Roubira, is a captivating storytelling game that challenges players to express their creativity and imagination. The game's dreamlike artwork, created by Marie Cardouat, transports players to a world of wonder and mystery.

The game's simple rules make it accessible to players of all ages, yet its depth lies in the unique way it encourages players to interpret the abstract artwork and create their own stories. Every card played becomes an opportunity for expression, leading to laughter, surprise, and endless fun.

Dixit's charm lies in its ability to bridge gaps and build connections between players through shared experiences. It has the power to turn strangers into storytellers and bring out the creativity in everyone around the table.
